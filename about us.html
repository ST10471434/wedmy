More About Us

Smile foundation was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between urban and rural communities and empower them through education, healthcare, and livelihood programs.
Founded in 2002 by Vikas Ranjan and a group of like-minded individuals with the goal of promoting education, healthcare, and sustainable livelihoods.
The organization began its work by focusing on education for underprivileged children, running programs to provide quality education in remote areas, particularly focusing on government schools and children who had little to no access to formal education.
Along with education, Smile Foundation expanded its programs to include healthcare and nutrition for children and families. They launched initiatives to provide essentials medical care, including health camps and nutritional support.


We have multiple programs that help in different areas of life, including:
Eduaction For All
Women Empowerment
Disaster Relief
Nutrition Programs
Community Development
